Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Our Commitment to Privacy

Image Toolkit processes all images entirely in your browser. Your files are never uploaded to any server. We do not collect, store, or transmit your images, nor do we have access to them.

How It Works

All image processing — conversion, compression, EXIF reading, color extraction, and Base64 encoding — is performed client-side using your browser's built-in APIs (Canvas, FileReader, etc.). At no point do your files leave your device.

Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this or other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

Analytics

We may use basic analytics to understand site traffic and improve our tools. No personal data is collected.

Contact

Questions? Create an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us via the details provided there.
