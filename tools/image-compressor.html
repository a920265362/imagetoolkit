How to Compress Images Online

  1. Drag and drop your image into the upload area
  2. Adjust the quality slider to balance file size and image quality
  3. Click "Compress" to see the preview
  4. Download your compressed image when satisfied

Pro tip: 70-80% quality typically reduces file size by 50-70% with minimal visible quality loss for JPG and WebP images. PNG compression preserves transparency but may not reduce size as dramatically.
