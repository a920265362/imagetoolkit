How to Use

  1. Upload a product photo
  2. Set scale — e.g., 100 pixels = 10 cm (measure a known reference on the photo)
  3. Choose mode — Length (horizontal), Height (vertical), or Diagonal
  4. Click two points on the image to draw the dimension line with arrows and label
  5. Adjust color, text size, or undo mistakes
  6. Download the final image. Perfect for Amazon, shop listings, spec sheets.
